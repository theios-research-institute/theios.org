====================================================================== WRITTEN RESOLUTION OF THE SOLE DIRECTOR OF THEIOS RESEARCH INSTITUTE, INC. (A Colorado Nonprofit Corporation) RESOLUTION NO. 2026-002 APPOINTMENT OF DIRECTOR — JOHN DEULLOA ====================================================================== DATE: April 14, 2026 The undersigned, being the sole Director of Theios Research Institute, Inc. (the "Corporation"), acting pursuant to Section 2.3(c) of the Bylaws (appointment of additional directors by sole director via written resolution), hereby adopts the following resolution: WHEREAS, the Corporation currently has one (1) director, Neil Sargisian, who serves as the Founding Director; WHEREAS, Section 2.2(a) of the Bylaws provides that the Board of Directors shall consist of not fewer than one (1) and not more than fifteen (15) directors; WHEREAS, the Founding Director deems it in the best interest of the Corporation to appoint an additional director to strengthen the governance and oversight of the Corporation; NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, that John DeUlloa is hereby appointed as a Director of Theios Research Institute, Inc., effective upon his execution of the Written Consent to Serve attached hereto. BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that John DeUlloa shall serve a term of three (3) years from the effective date of this resolution, or until his successor is elected and qualified, or until his earlier resignation or removal, in accordance with Article II of the Bylaws as amended by Resolution No. 2026-001. BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that John DeUlloa shall serve without compensation for his service as a Director, consistent with Section 2.6 of the Bylaws, but may be reimbursed for reasonable expenses incurred in the performance of his duties, subject to Board approval. BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that John DeUlloa shall be provided with a copy of the Corporation's current Bylaws (as amended), Articles of Incorporation, and Conflict of Interest Policy, and shall be required to execute the annual Conflict of Interest Acknowledgment as set forth in Section 6.8 of the Bylaws. ______________________________ Neil Sargisian Founding Director and President Theios Research Institute, Inc.
