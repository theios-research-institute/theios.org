About Your Donation

Theios Research Institute, Inc. is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization (EIN: 41-3302025). Your donation is tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law. You will receive a receipt via email.

For donations over $5,000 or to discuss other giving options, please contact research@theios.org.

Why It Matters

Independent, donor-funded research allows us to follow questions wherever they lead — and to share what we discover openly.

Your support makes this possible.

What Your Donation Supports

Discovery Science: Computational research revealing structural regularities across domains.

Open Knowledge: Public briefs and reproducible research artifacts freely available to all.

Research Infrastructure: Tools and systems that enable rigorous, protocol-governed validation.

Knowledge-system Engineering: Building structured systems that organize and connect understanding across disciplines.

Epistemic Framework Design: Developing rigorous methods for evaluating claims and establishing justified belief.

Independence: Donor support allows us to pursue questions without institutional constraints.

Thank you for believing in the value of independent inquiry.
